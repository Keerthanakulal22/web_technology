Institute Achieves Autonomous Status

Shri Madhwa Vadiraj Institute of Technology and Management has officially been granted autonomous status, marking a significant milestone in its academic journey. This transition empowers the institute to design its own curriculum, conduct examinations, and foster innovation in teaching and research.
